My Projects

Sarap Norte

A digital cookbook app dedicated to showcasing the rich and flavorful cuisine of Camarines Norte. It features traditional local dishes, complete with descriptions, photos, and easy-to-follow instructions. The goal of Sarap Norte is to preserve and celebrate regional Filipino flavors while making them accessible to the modern cook.

Dish n’ Co

Your cozy kitchen companion for discovering and creating delicious meals. Dish n’ Co helps users find inspiration and enjoy cooking through a wide collection of recipes for every taste and occasion, from quick weekday meals to comforting favorites.

BEEKEEPER’S ASSOCIATION OF CAMARINES NORTE (BACN)

The Beekeepers Association of Camarines Norte (BACN) is an organization dedicated to promoting sustainable beekeeping and developing bee-based products within the province. The association produces health and wellness items such as soaps, shampoos, colognes, vapor rubs, essential oils, and raw honey. These products are valued for their natural ingredients and eco-friendly benefits.
